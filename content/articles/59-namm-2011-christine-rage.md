# NAMM 2011 / Christine Rage

**Article ID:** 59

Coming January 13-16, Nikola will be appearing at the winter NAMM music convention alongside recording artist and Randall Amps endorser Christine Rage. Nikola is very pleased to announce that he will be collaborating with the multi-talented Christine Rage and is scheduled to perform lead guitar on her upcoming recordings.

To find out more about Christine Rage, please visit her website www.christinerage.com
