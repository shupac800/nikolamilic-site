# EP Release 2011

**Article ID:** 58

Nikola is currently working on finishing recordings for his long awaited EP tentatively due out for release early 2011. After numerous production setbacks and delays involving line up changes and unforeseen studio relocations, Nikola is back in the saddle and will be recording all guitars, bass and keyboards along with the engineering aid of Dave Shuman.

The EP will feature all instrumental tracks written and arranged by Nikola Milic and will appeal to a variety of rock and metal fans. A must for any serious guitarist and a shredders delight, once released the EP will be available on iTunes, Amazon.com and all other major stores worldwide!
