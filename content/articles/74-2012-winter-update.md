# 2012 Winter Update

**Article ID:** 74

Seasons Greetings! To all my friends, fellow musicians, students and fans who aren't connected with me via Facebook or Twitter, I'd like to fill y'all in on the latest news!

As some of you may know, my 2011 solo recordings were put on hold, which was necessary due to my busy touring schedule with the band Dust N' Bones. I'd like to thank everyone who came out to support me and the band live, as well as all those who voted for Dust N' Bones as the Vegas Rocks Magazine Best Tribute Band nominee of 2011! It was quite an experience!

I decided to leave the band a while back, as I wanted to continue work on my solo material as well as other projects. I'm pleased to announce that I'm currently a Kahler Tremolo endorser and have also teamed up with Leigh Guitars, who are currently working with me on the production of a custom Nikola Milic signature model guitar. The new axe will feature a 36 fret, 3 octave, fully scalloped fretboard, designed for some serious shredding! The first prototype will be complete sometime by February of 2013.

As always, I will be appearing at the winter NAMM convention in Anaheim, and this time you can find me jamming out at the Kahler Trem booth! Until then, here are some live clips from my stint with Dust N' Bones...

Cheers!
